STYLE: Information / explainer video.

- Mood: clear, friendly, professional.
- Visual style: clean, well-lit, modern; uncluttered scenes that support the narration.
- Camera: steady with subtle motion (gentle push-in, slow pan).
- Each shot should clearly show the concept being narrated, with one focal subject.
- Default negative prompt:
  text, subtitles, watermark, logo, low resolution, blurry, distorted anatomy, cluttered scene, frame border
